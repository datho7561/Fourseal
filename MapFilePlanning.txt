WHAT THE NUMBERS CORRESPOND TO:
(All sets inclusive at both bounds)

0: Empty (i.e. no tile)

1-8: The background tiles

9-15: The foreground tiles

16-19: The decoration tiles

20+: Special tiles

STRUCTURE:

There first three sets consist of comma separated values

The first 9 rows represent the background sprites

The next 9 rows represent the foreground sprites

The next 9 rows represent the decoration sprites

The next 9 rows represent placement of key game objects

The next rows represent how the runes are linked
